About the Ministry

Brides of Yeshua was created by a young woman who loves Yahuah more than anyone could ever imagine! The name, inspired by scripture, details how we are to be married unto Christ and continue in marriage walking a life pleasing to our Father through His perfect example.

The main goal is to spread the true message of scriptures through using scripture itself, all while adding a cute, girly touch to it (who doesn't love pink?!). Though primarily created with women in mind, we welcome men too, for the Word isn't only for one gender, but both!
